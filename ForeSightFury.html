Stages and Timelines

  • Round 1

    All of the registered teams will solve the given problem statement and submit a PPT. Go to http://www.coesea.iitkgp.ac.in/shadg24/workshop.php for the Problem Statement and other updated documents.

  • Start Date 1 Jan 24, 12:00 AM IST
  • End Date 27 Jan 24, 5:00 PM IST
  • Round 2(Offline)

    Shortlisted teams will be invited to present their Round 1 solutions and strategies to a panel of judges in a live setting. This presentation should highlight the practicality and potential impact of their proposed solutions. Teams must develop a feasible solution model using various software, open-source libraries or other relevant tools, pertaining to the provided problem statement. The presentation will be for 10 minutes followed by an 5-minute Q&A session. Evaluation will be based on operations acumen, feasibility in implementing the ideation, presentation skills, and problem modelling.

  • Start Date 29 Jan 24, 09:30 AM IST

Rules

Judges will assess the quality of the case presentation, the effectiveness of the models, and the overall feasibility and impact of the proposed solutions. Teams will be scored based on their ability to solve the given problem , feasibility of the solution & presentation.

  • The registrations will close by 05:00 PM, 27th January 2024.
  • Teams must comprise of a minimum 1 and maximum 2 members, all of whom must be enrolled in a graduate or a post-graduate program at the time of the submission.
  • Cross-college teams are allowed.
